📖 Rules of Pub Golf

Welcome to Pub Golf — where the course is a pub crawl and every sip counts. The aim is to finish your designated drink in as few sips as possible. Under par is good. Over par is bad. Just like real golf.

The Basics

  • ⛳Each venue is a hole. The number of holes is set by the organiser (up to 18).
  • 🍺Each hole has a designated drink you must consume.
  • 🔢Each hole has a par — the target number of sips to finish your drink.
  • 👆Every sip = 1 stroke. Fewer sips than par → under par. More sips → over par.
  • 🚫You must not put your drink down between sips.
  • ✅The drink must be fully finished before leaving a hole.

Special Holes

  • 🌊Water Hole — No one in the team may use the bathroom for the entire duration of this hole.
  • ⛱️Bunker Hole — Every team member must take an extra shot of spirits before moving on.

Penalties

  • ⚡Penalty types are set by the organiser before the game starts.
  • 🤢Common penalties: vomiting (+3), spilling drink (+2), phone out during hole (+1).

Dress Code

  • 👔Golf attire is strongly encouraged — polo shirts, chinos, golf caps, visors.
🍻 Above all — drink responsibly, look after your team, and have an absolute blast. Cheers!
